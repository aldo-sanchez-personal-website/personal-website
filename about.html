Whenever a project or workday is over, I immerse myself in my favorite hobbies.

Gaming!

Online gaming is one of the most popular hobbies among young adults and I'm no exception! I enjoy spending my downtime after a long day with video games. I like to play classic multiplayer games such as Left 4 Dead 2, Team fortress 2, and OldSchool Runescape. Games are great way to release stress and I find that it never fails to put me in a good mood!

Birdwatching!

Another hobby I love to do during the spring and summer is going out at dawn to the local lakeshore trail to birdwatch. I always found nature to be fascinating and beautiful. Gazing at the wildlife and seeing nature at play aalways brings me a sense of peace and relaxation, It is a very welcome distraction from the hustle and bustle of city life.
